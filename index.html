I follow a systematic and empathetic process to ensure a comprehensive understanding of users, which ultimately informs good product design and development decisions.

Methodologies: User interviews & surveys, usability testing, competitor analysis, data analysis

Methodologies: User empathy, wireframing, information architecture, user flow, visual design, consistency & branding, accessibility & inclusivity, interaction design, prototyping, responsive design, user & usability testing, iterative process, cross-collaboration

Mobile UI & UX Design

Travel solo with the pursuit of happiness.

Gourmade

Mobile UI & UX Design

The Gourmade app is designed to make cooking easy and fun. It provides a personalized cooking experience for each user. Through feedback loops our app learns about the users' cooking habits, generates a weekly meal plan with a complete with grocery list, schedule, cost breakdown, and videos. Has a discover page to inspire user's cooking and can save recipes for future use. We remove the barriers to home cooking and empower users to cook in a way that fits their lifestyle.
